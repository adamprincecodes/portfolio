EDEN RESTAURANT MENU

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Samosa

    • Crispy triangular pastries filled with spiced minced beef or chicken, or vegetables like peas and potatoes, then deep fried.

  • Mandazi

    • Slightly sweet fried dough made from flour, sugar, coconut milk (or milk), and cardamom.

  • Bhajia

    • Thin slices of potatoes coated in a seasoned gram flour, batter with turmeric and chili.


Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    • Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Nyama Choma

    • Roasted or grilled meat, seasoned with salt and slow-cooked over an open flame, often served with kachumbari.

  • Pilau

    • Fragrant rice cooked with meat, garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, and cloves.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    • Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Carrot Cake

    • Moist cake made with grated carrots, warm spices like cinnamon, and often walnuts or raisins, topped with cream cheese frosting.

  • Vanilla Cake

    • A simple, soft cake made with flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla, often topped with light vanilla frosting or served plain.
